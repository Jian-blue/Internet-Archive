Launch of YouTube
2005
YouTube was founded and became a major video sharing platform.

First Viral Meme
2007
Rickrolling becomes a famous internet prank and meme.

Nyan Cat Meme
2011
A looping animated cat with a Pop-Tart body flying through space becomes a viral internet meme.

Facebook IPO
2012
Facebook goes public with one of the largest tech IPOs in history.

Google Becomes Alphabet
2015
Google restructures itself under a new parent company called Alphabet Inc.

Dogecoin Meme Surge
2021
Dogecoin becomes a viral cryptocurrency, supported by memes.

Launch of ChatGPT
2022
OpenAI releases ChatGPT, sparking a boom in public AI awareness.
